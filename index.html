About Steel Titans

Our Journey

The Steel Titans started their rookie season in 2024 at Claremont International High School. Since then, our team has been dedicated to building, programming, and competing with innovative robots in FTC competitions. We believe in teamwork, learning by doing, and inspiring creativity through engineering.
